Test Your Understanding

For each chapter, we have developed a test to help you evaluate your understanding of the basic concepts and techniques covered in that chapter.

Each chapter test is composed of five questions, selected randomly from a large set of questions associated with that chapter. Hence the test may be retaken multiple times.

Your Score: For each question, if you answer it correctly on your first attempt, you receive 20 points. A correct answer on the second attempt yields 10 points, and no points are realized after the second attempt.
